(`/charly-internals:go` "Generation coverage"): **ALL wire types — the
`sdk/spec/*_wire.go` host↔plugin / render-context data-carrier structs + every
plugin's params — are CUE-sourced in `sdk/schema/*.cue` and generated by

(`hand_state_types.go`) — each `@go(-)`'d, a LANGUAGE limit, never a discretionary
choice; a new wire type is designed to avoid them (a discriminated `Kind`+optionals
struct over a disjunction, an explicit map field over an open tail). Reframes the
former "deploy_wire.go — classified candidate for future schema-sourcing" as: any
still-hand-written `*_wire.go` is a conversion-in-progress under the mandate, NOT a
sanctioned exception. Pairs with the CLAUDE.md SDD-pillar mandate (superproject).
