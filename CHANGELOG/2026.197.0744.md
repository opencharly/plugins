# 2026.197.0744 — Validator handoff

The PR-validator policy records a complete parent handoff in the clean author worktree:
the target protected base and PR head, protected-policy object, repository/gitlink map,
approval categories, and mutation limits. A fresh validator uses that handoff to load
protected policy and skills before candidate actions, treating an unavailable required
object, capability, or declared gitlink as a durable `BLOCKED` result.

The validator does not bootstrap an additional checkout or rely on a helper task,
`/tmp` ledger, user-level setup, or a guessed relationship between a submodule's target
base and the superproject gitlink. Its verdict is project-local validation state.
